Bottleneck Zero Blueprint

The exact system to convert more existing leads without ad spend increase.

Core Mechanism

Most businesses leak leads in the first 30 minutes after inbound. We fix that with speed + relevance + one clear next step.

Pillar 1: Speed-to-Lead

Reply in under 5 minutes with context and one question.

  • Bad: long pitch
  • Good: short personalized opener

Pillar 2: Objection Clarity

“Too expensive” usually means “I’m not sure this will work.”

  • Use outcome + timeframe + proof
  • Ask for next step

Pillar 3: Ghost Revival

Re-open dead chats with a value-first sequence over 72 hours.

Pillar 4: Daily Distribution

One short post per day teaching one micro-strategy linked to your offer.

Daily Operating System (30 mins/day)

  • 10 min: publish one value post
  • 10 min: reply to all buyer-intent comments
  • 10 min: reactivation batch from old leads

Success Benchmarks

  • First response median under 10 min
  • Reply rate above 20%
  • Booked calls up week over week
