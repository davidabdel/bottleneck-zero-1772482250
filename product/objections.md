# Handling Price Objections

## Core principle
Most objections reflect uncertainty, not rejection.

Use this structure:
- Outcome (what changes)
- Timeframe (when it should change)
- Proof (why this is credible)

## Examples
### "Too expensive"
Totally fair. If we could improve [metric] in [timeframe], would this feel like a clear yes?

### "Need to think about it"
Makes sense. Want a one-page implementation map so your decision is based on clear steps, not guesswork?

## Do / Don’t
- ✅ Ask clarifying questions
- ✅ Keep responses short and specific
- ❌ Argue emotionally
- ❌ Offer discounts immediately
