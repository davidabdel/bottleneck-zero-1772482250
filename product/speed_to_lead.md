# Speed-to-Lead Framework

## Target
Reply to new leads in under 10 minutes (ideal: under 5 minutes).

## Message formula
1. Acknowledge context quickly
2. Clarify their primary outcome
3. Offer one simple next step

## Example
Hey [Name] — saw your message about [topic].
Are you currently trying to solve [pain A] or [pain B] first?
Once I know that, I can share the fastest path.

## Operational notes
- Use snippets to reduce typing delay
- Avoid paragraphs longer than 2–3 lines
- Track response time and reply rate weekly

See `scripts_library.md` for ready-to-use variants.
